Mylink.id is a place for people to share their expertise, advice & knowledge with everyone through our link.

How does Mylink.id work?

You can put any link of your preferred medium (telegram/whatsapp/etc) in your lynk.id page. Your audience will need to pay the price you set before they interact with you.
